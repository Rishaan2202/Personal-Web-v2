About Me

My Journey

I have had a really wonderful journey till where i am now! First of all, I was born in Jaipur, Rajasthan. My father had a job in Mundra, Kutchh, Gujarat, so I moved there after my birth! I lived there for 13.5 Years of Mine and have a lot of memories attached with that place! Then we moved to Nagpur, Maharashtra because my father got transferred there! And yea, that's where I am now :)

Education

I studied at various schools, including Calorex Public School in Mundra, Kutchh, and Adani Public School in the same location. Currently I study at Delhi Public School in Mihan, Nagpur.
